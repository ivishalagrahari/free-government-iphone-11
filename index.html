Eligibility Criteria

To participate in this benefit program, an applicant must demonstrate qualification through Income or Program Participation.

1. Federal Benefit Programs

  • Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program (SNAP)
  • Medicaid / Medi-Cal
  • Supplemental Security Income (SSI)
  • Federal Public Housing Assistance (FPHA)
  • Veterans Pension and Survivors Benefit

2. Income Guidelines

Households with a gross annual income at or below 135% of the Federal Poverty Guidelines are eligible.

Application Process

Follow these standard operating procedures (SOP) to submit your claim.

  1. Verification: Navigate to the National Verifier or the provider's portal to confirm your eligibility status.
  2. Selection: Choose the "iPhone 11" from the device catalog. Note that stock fluctuates daily.
  3. Submission: Upload clear photographs of your proof documents (ID + Benefit Letter).
  4. Activation: Once received, insert the provided SIM card to activate service.
